Cloud
  In meteorolgy, a cloud is an aerosol consisting of a visible mass of minute liquid droplets, frozen crystals, or other
  particles suspend in the atmosphere of a planetary body or similar space.They are seen in the Earth's homosphere, which includes the troposphere, stratosphere, and mesosphere. Nephology is the science of clouds, which is undertaken in the cloud physics branch of meteorology. There are two methods of naming clouds in their respective layers of the homosphere, Latin and common.
